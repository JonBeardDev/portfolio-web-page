About Me

After graduating from The University of Birmingham with a Bachelor's in Computer Science, circumstances, including a move to the US precluded me from pursuing a career as a developer. I spent many years as a 5th grade teacher of Math and Science, but the COVID-19 pandemic made me re-evaluate my career choices.

Strangely enough, it was through creating formulas and macros on a Google Sheet to track movie ratings that made me realize where my true passion lies. As a student at Thinkful I am enjoying my return to the world of coding. Better late than never, right?

If I'm not working on code, you'll typically find me solving puzzles, learning a new craft such as crocheting, or fostering kittens for my local animal shelter.
